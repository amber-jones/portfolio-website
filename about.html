Hi, I'm Amber.

I am a 23 year old creative based in Cape Town, South Africa. I've always had a love for art and design. I recently found my passion for web design and development whilst studying at The Academy of Digital Arts. After completing my BSc. in Chemistry and Genetics, I realized I did not want to take that route - I needed to express my creative side. Front end development is great in that one needs to think practically and analytically but the creative element (excuse the pun), plays a big part too.

A digital creative.

I love learning about new design trends and trying to execute them through my work. I am excited to join the front end development community and keep up with the latest trends and technologies. When I'm not in front of a screen, I am either outdoors - enjoying nature with friends/keeping active or drinking coffee, eating sushi and petting strangers' dogs.

Skills.

UI/UX Design

Structuring and designing user interfaces for e-commerce, corporate websites and landing pages that fully express your brand's identity, always keeping the user in mind. I use softwares such as Figma and Adobe Illustrator to help me design and prototype.

Front-End Development

Building user-friendly, responsive websites using HTML,CSS and JavaScript or through a content management system such as WordPress. Creating simple interactions for a seamless user experience.
